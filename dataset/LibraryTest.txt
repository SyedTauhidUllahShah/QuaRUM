Library Management System - Test Requirements
Version 1.0

1. Patron Management
The library system must support multiple patron types including students, faculty, and community members. Each patron can borrow up to 10 books simultaneously. Each patron is assigned a unique library card number. Patrons must be able to place holds on books that are currently checked out. Some sections refer to a patron as a LibraryUser; treat this as the same concept as Patron.

2. Book Management
The system shall maintain a catalog of books, where each book has a title, ISBN, author, and publication year. Each book record tracks the number of available copies. Checked-out books have a due date and may be renewed once.

3. Catalog Management
The library shall maintain a catalog that organizes all books available for borrowing. The catalog must support searching books by title, author, and ISBN.

4. Loan Management
The system shall record each loan with a loan date, due date, and return date. A patron may renew a loan once if no hold exists on the book. The system shall generate overdue notices for loans that exceed the due date.
